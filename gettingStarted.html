What happens on a turn

A turn is made up of five phases which are:
  • Beginning phase
  • First main phase
  • Combat phase
  • Second main phase
  • Ending phase

Beginning phase

  • Untap step
  • Upkeep step
  • Draw step

First main phase

Player can cast, activiate avilities, play one land per turn. Apponent can activate abilities and cast instants.

Combat phase

  • Beginning of combat step
  • Declare attackers step
  • Declare blockers step
  • Combat damage step
  • End of combat step
