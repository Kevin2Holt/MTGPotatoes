Getting Started

Welcome to the exciting world of Magic: The Gathering! Whether you're a newcomer to the game or a seasoned gamer looking to learn a new hobby, this guide will help you take your first steps into the multiverse of Magic.

This guide hopes to provide you with enough information to get you started playing your first game. I also hope that it can be a reference for you as you get to know the game.

I separated the game into several key parts that break down the beefy game into manageable, bite-sized chunks that are easier to understand and quick to remember.

What to Expect:

Card Overview

This section will provide an overview of what you’ll find on a card as well as some brief explanations of different game mechanics.

Card Types

The different card types play in different ways. This section goes more in-depth about each, explaining how each works and what to do with each one.

Board Zones

To someone who doesn’t understand, the table generally looks like a mess during a game. This section explains the different zones used in the game along with some common setups.

Turn Overview

Once you have some basic knowledge of the cards and how they work, you’ll need to know what happens during each turn. This section lays out every part of a turn so you can know when you can play cards and how you win.
